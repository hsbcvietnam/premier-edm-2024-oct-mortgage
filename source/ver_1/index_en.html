Dear Valued Premier customer [NAME],
 
We would like to inform you regarding an update to the eligibility criteria for maintaining HSBC Premier status
 
From November 20th, 2024 the Premier eligibility by Home Loan/Mortgage Criteria will beadjusted as following:

The "HSBC Premier Home Loan/Mortgage Criteria" customer tier is applicable to customers who meet the following conditions (hereinafter referred to as "Premier Customer" or "Customer"):
(i) Customers who have a home/mortgage loan with a minimum outstanding balance of VND 5 billion; and
(ii) Loan amount less than 60% of the value of the mortgaged property and
(iii) Monthly salary credits to HSBC accounts, and
(iv) After the trial period, customer is required to meet Total Relationship Balance criteria(*) to continue being HSBC Premier Customer.
The Trial Period applicable for HSBC Premier Home Loan/Mortgage Criteria" customer is effective for 12 consecutive months from the time the customer successfully opens an account or upgrades to HSBC Premier services,(hereinafter referred to as the "Trial Period"). During the Trial Period, customer is not required to meet Total Relationship Balance criteria to being HSBC Premier Customer.
 
 
Please note that the Premier eligibility by Total Relationship Balance (TRB)(*) and by Salary criteria remain unchanged. So, if you are already fulfilling one of the following criteria, your Premier eligibility will continue and there is no action required from your side:
• Maintain a minimum average monthly Total Relationship Balance (TRB)(*) of VND 1 billion (or equivalent foreign currency value);
• Receive a monthly salary of at least VND 100 million (or equivalent foreign currency value) into your HSBC Vietnam account
 
(*)Applicable products for TRB calculation include: Deposits (minimum of VND 700 million or FCY equivalent), Mutual Funds and Life Insurance. All product must remain valid and active at the time of TRB assessment.
